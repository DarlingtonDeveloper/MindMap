# Audience Overview

This blog will grow over time, but right now, the content will be surfaced first to people who already follow me on different platforms. Understanding where they're coming from helps guide what I say, how I say it, and where to start the journey.

## 👥 Current Audience Groups

### 1. Crypto Curious Builders (Twitter)
- Developers and founders active in Web3 communities
- Followed me from Twitter Spaces or Web3 conferences
- Interested in DAOs, DePIN, token ecosystems
- Overstimulated, context-switching, and hungry for clarity

### 2. Inspired Wanderers (Instagram)
- Friends, travelers, creatives I’ve met on the road
- Care more about lifestyle, alignment, and energy than code
- Likely to resonate with wellness, design, or life philosophy content

### 3. Platform-Watching Engineers (LinkedIn)
- Tech recruiters and engineers who connected passively
- May not know what I’m up to, but curious if it connects to careers or tools
- Potential readers for practical, career-adjacent content

## 🎯 Ideal Audience Trajectory

The goal is to move readers toward:
- **The Aligned Dev** → Reflective, skilled, and seeking clarity
- **The Aligned Builder** → Energized, focused, and building on their own terms

This shift is what the blog (and later, tools like SoloBoost) will gently support.
