# 🧠 Michael’s Second Brain – Index

Welcome to your refreshed Obsidian Vault — structured to support your modular AI systems, personal development, and ongoing projects.

---

## 📂 Folder Overview

### 📁 00 Inbox
Quick capture zone for raw notes, voice logs, or ideas.

### 📁 01 Journals
Daily logs, mood tracking, and agent-linked reflections.

### 📁 02 Projects
Active builds like **Sprout**, **Oru**, and your **Docs** site.

### 📁 03 Systems
Long-term life and work systems: focus, health, learning, distraction, and agent behaviors.

### 📁 04 Knowledge
Evergreen notes from curated readings, tools, and personal insights.

### 📁 05 Archive
Retired roles, deprecated systems, and past projects.

### 📁 06 Reviews
Weekly, monthly, and quarterly reflections with Observer summaries and prompts.

### 📁 07 Thinking
Your sandbox for developing ideas, mechanisms, agent designs, and conceptual explorations.

### 📁 08 Writing
Public-facing writing, including blog drafts, essays, and the Sprout Manifesto.

### 📁 09 Library
Curated media — books, papers, videos, articles, and podcasts.

### 📁 10 Kanban
Visual task boards for agents, rituals, and weekly sprints (uses the Obsidian Kanban plugin).

## 🌱 Getting Started

1. Use the Daily Note Template in `01 Journals/Daily/`
2. Update your agent task board in `10 Kanban/Sprout – Agent Task Board.md`
